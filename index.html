// The Beginning

It started with curiosity — a teenager in India who couldn't stop asking "how does this website actually work?" That question led me down a rabbit hole of HTML, CSS, and late-night YouTube tutorials. I was hooked. What began as tinkering quickly became an obsession, and I knew this was what I wanted to build my life around.

// The Journey

My journey in tech started with curiosity and quickly turned into a passion for building and learning. Over the past 2+ years, I've focused on mastering full-stack development, cloud computing, and AI, while continuously challenging myself to grow.

From solving 200+ LeetCode problems to achieving Arcade Diamond League on Google Skills Boost, I've stayed consistent in improving both my technical and problem-solving skills. Becoming an AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate marked a key milestone in my journey toward building scalable cloud solutions.

I'm driven by one goal — to build technology that feels simple, powerful, and meaningful to users.

// Right Now

Today, I work on cloud-native applications, focusing on scalable systems, databases, and deployment. I use Docker and Amazon Web Services for DevOps and cloud infrastructure, with a growing interest in integrating AI into real-world solutions.

I'm open to freelance work and full-time opportunities to build impactful systems.

// Beyond the Code

When I'm not pushing commits, I'm mentoring aspiring developers, exploring open-source, or deep in a rabbit hole of new tech. I believe great software is equal parts logic and empathy — and I bring both to everything I build.
